## What This App Does
OneCart UI is the customer-facing booking engine where hotel guests:
1. Search availability (rooms, activities, spa, golf, etc.)
2. Add items to a cart
3. Check out and pay

## Who Uses It
- **End users:** Hotel guests booking online
- **Tenants:** Each hotel/resort is a "tenant" with custom branding
